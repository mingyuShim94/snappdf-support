Privacy Policy

SnapPDF - Photo to PDF Maker
Last updated: February 16, 2026

SnapPDF does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your photos and PDFs never leave your device.

Overview

SnapPDF ("the App") is developed by gguggulab. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Data Collection

SnapPDF does not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • No usage analytics or tracking data is collected
  • No photos or documents are uploaded to any server
  • No cookies or identifiers are used
  • No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs are included

How the App Works

All photo-to-PDF conversion is performed entirely on your device. The App:

  • Accesses your photo library only when you explicitly select photos to convert
  • Accesses your camera only when you choose to take a photo for conversion
  • Stores converted PDFs locally on your device
  • Does not require an internet connection to function

Photo Library & Camera Access

The App requests access to your photo library and camera solely for the purpose of selecting or capturing images to convert to PDF. These permissions are optional and requested only when needed. No images are transmitted off your device.

Data Storage

Converted PDF files and conversion history are stored locally on your device within the App's sandbox. This data is automatically removed when you delete the App.

Third-Party Services

SnapPDF does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from any user, including children under 13. The App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
