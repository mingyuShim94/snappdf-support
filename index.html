Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert photos to PDF?

Open SnapPDF, tap "Photos" to select images from your gallery, customize page size and orientation if needed, then tap "Convert to PDF". Your PDF is ready to share.

Does SnapPDF require an internet connection?

No. SnapPDF works completely offline. All conversion happens on your device â€” your files are never uploaded to any server.

What page sizes are supported?

SnapPDF supports A4, US Letter, and Legal page sizes in both portrait and landscape orientation. You can also adjust margins (None, Small, Medium, Large).

Where are my converted PDFs saved?

All converted PDFs are saved in the app's History tab. You can search, sort by date, and share them anytime. PDFs can also be shared directly after conversion via AirDrop, Mail, Messages, and more.

Is SnapPDF really free?

Yes. SnapPDF is free to use with no watermarks, no sign-up, and no hidden fees.

What image formats are supported?

SnapPDF supports JPEG, PNG, HEIC, and other common image formats that iOS supports.
